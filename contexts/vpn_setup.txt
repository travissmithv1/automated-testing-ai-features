VPN Setup Instructions

The company VPN (Virtual Private Network) allows you to securely access company resources when working remotely.

VPN Software Installation:

Windows:
1. Download Cisco AnyConnect from the IT Portal: https://it.company.com/vpn
2. Run the installer
3. Follow the installation wizard
4. Restart your computer when prompted

Mac:
1. Download Cisco AnyConnect from the IT Portal: https://it.company.com/vpn
2. Open the .dmg file
3. Double-click the installer package
4. Follow the installation wizard
5. Restart your computer when prompted

Connecting to VPN:

1. Open Cisco AnyConnect
2. Enter VPN address: vpn.company.com
3. Click Connect
4. Enter your employee ID
5. Enter your password
6. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app (Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator)
7. Click OK

Setting Up Two-Factor Authentication:
1. Download Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator on your mobile device
2. Log into the IT Portal: https://it.company.com
3. Go to Security Settings
4. Click "Setup 2FA"
5. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
6. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
7. Save your backup codes in a secure location

When to Use VPN:
- Working from home
- Accessing shared drives remotely
- Accessing company email from non-company devices
- Any time you need to access internal company resources outside the office

VPN is NOT required:
- When physically in the office
- For accessing public company website
- For general internet browsing

Troubleshooting:
- If connection fails, verify your internet connection is working
- Make sure you're entering the current 6-digit code (they expire every 30 seconds)
- If you lost access to your authenticator app, contact IT support immediately
- VPN may take up to 48 hours to activate for new employees

For VPN support, contact IT helpdesk at x4357 or itsupport@company.com
